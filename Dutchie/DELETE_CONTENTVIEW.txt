⚠️ CRITICAL STEP - READ THIS FIRST ⚠️

YOU MUST DELETE THE DEFAULT ContentView.swift FILE!

In Xcode's left sidebar, you should see:
- DutchieApp.swift (keep this)
- ContentView.swift (DELETE THIS ONE!)

Right-click on ContentView.swift → Delete → Move to Trash

If you don't delete it, you'll get "Invalid redeclaration of ContentView" error.

---

ALSO VERIFY: Make sure you created an iOS App project!

Go to your project settings (click the blue Dutchie icon at the top):
- Under TARGETS → General
- Check "Supported Destinations" - it should say "iPhone" and "iPad"
- If it says "Mac" or "My Mac", you created a macOS project by mistake
  → You need to start over and create an iOS App project

---

After deleting ContentView.swift and confirming iOS target, build again.
